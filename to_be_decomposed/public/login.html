Privacy Policy

Welcome to the ft_transcendence, developed as part of the 42 school curriculum. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently and securely. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use this application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Username, display name, avatar (if provided), and encrypted password.
  • Gameplay Data: Match history, scores, win/loss statistics, rankings, achievements, and in-game actions.
  • Interaction Data: Chat messages sent within the app, friend lists, block lists, and reports submitted.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referral URLs, and timestamps.
We do not collect sensitive information such as your real name, email address, phone number, or payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to operate and improve the app, enable gameplay features, moderate interactions, enforce rules, troubleshoot issues, and manage user accounts. We do not sell or share your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies or localStorage to remember your login session, store interface preferences, and maintain your authentication status. You may clear these from your browser settings, but doing so may log you out or reset preferences.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following cases: (a) compliance with legal obligations, (b) protection of the app’s security and integrity, (c) investigation of violations of our terms.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active. If your account is inactive for an extended period, we may delete or anonymize your data. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the project team.

6. Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your data, such as encrypted password storage, secure connections (HTTPS), and access controls. However, no system is completely secure; please protect your credentials.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR). To exercise these rights, contact us at [team_email@example.com]. We may require verification of your identity before fulfilling your request.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users by posting an announcement in the app. Please review this policy periodically for updates.

By using this app, you acknowledge and agree to this policy.
