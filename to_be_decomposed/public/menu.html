How to Play Coderush

Coderush is a fast-paced typing game where players compete to type and submit words as quickly as possible. There are two modes: Single Player and Multiplayer.

Single Player Mode:

  • Compete against the clock to type and submit as many words as possible.
  • Use the Enter or Spacebar to submit each word.
  • Earn points for accuracy and speed.

Multiplayer Mode:

  • Challenge your friends or other players online.
  • Type and submit words faster than your opponents to win.
  • Use the Enter or Spacebar to submit each word.
  • The player with the highest score at the end of the round wins.

Remember: Speed and accuracy are key to mastering Coderush. Good luck!
